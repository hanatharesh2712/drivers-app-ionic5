5. Compensation

DRVN agrees to compensate the Partner a Fee for each Ride successfully completed. A Ride is deemed successfully completed when the Partner picks-up the Passenger and drops-off the Passenger at the arranged destination. The Partner acknowledges and agrees to comply with the Fee posted by DRVN for each Ride, unless, DRVN and the Partner agree in writing to a specific Fee amount. The Rate shall be proportionally adjusted if any modifications are made to the Ride Offer and/or Ride.

5.2 Ride Settlement and Payment Execution
